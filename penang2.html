Curtis crest tree top walk




Tour Detail

Bukit Bendera, Penang, Malaysia

06:30a.m-10:00p.m

Description

Curtis Crest Tree Top Walk, a popular attraction located in The Habitat Penang Hill, Malaysia. The Habitat Penang Hill is a nature park and conservation area situated on the slopes of Penang Hill, offering visitors the chance to explore the rainforest canopy via a series of elevated walkways and platforms.

The Curtis Crest Tree Top Walk is one of the highlights of The Habitat Penang Hill experience. It is a suspended walkway that takes visitors to a panoramic viewing platform known as Curtis Crest, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding rainforest and George Town in the distance.

The walk to Curtis Crest takes visitors through the lush rainforest canopy, providing opportunities to observe diverse flora and fauna along the way. The elevated walkway is designed to minimize impact on the environment while allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of the rainforest from a unique vantage point.

At Curtis Crest, visitors can enjoy stunning views of the forest canopy, as well as panoramic vistas of Penang Island and the Strait of Malacca beyond. It's a great spot for photography, birdwatching, or simply soaking in the natural beauty of the area.

The Curtis Crest Tree Top Walk is typically included as part of The Habitat Penang Hill experience, which also includes guided nature walks, educational exhibits, and other attractions aimed at promoting environmental conservation and awareness.

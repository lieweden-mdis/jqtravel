Sarawak
Sarawak, the largest state in Malaysia, is situated on the northwest of Borneo. Renowned for its rich cultural tapestry, diverse indigenous tribes, and stunning natural wonders, Sarawak offers a captivating blend of adventure and heritage. Sarawak beckons travelers to explore its unique landscapes and immerse themselves in its fascinating history.  

Attractions

Sarawak Cultural Village
Sarawak Cultural Village, “Learn about Sarawak in half a day”, is Sarawak Cultural Village’s commitment to every tourist. It vividly displays and introduces the local culture and living customs of Sarawak. It is an award-winning living museum and is the perfect place to visit. A must-see attraction for travelers to Sarawak!

Pantai Damai, Santubong 93752 Kuching Sarawak
09:00a.m-04:45p.m
View Details
Kuching Cat Museum
An attraction that must not be missed when coming to Sarawak is the world's first cat museum, which displays more than 4,000 exhibits, paintings, photos, artworks and souvenirs about cat owners. Everything related to cats can be seen here. arrive! In addition to cat life, it also introduces cats in different regions and cultures, cat graves, bloodlines, art, cartoons, etc. It is very creative and interesting!

Bangunan DBKU, Jalan Semariang 93050 Kuching Sarawak
09:00a.m-05:00p.m
View Details
Jong’s Crocodile Farm
This is the first and largest crocodile farm in Malaysia. It houses thousands of crocodiles at the same time and attracts many foreign TV stations to come and film. In addition to crocodiles, there are other unique small animals.

OT80, Jalan Taman Buaya Jong, SIBURAN, 29KM(18.5MILES), KUCHING SERIAN HIGHWAY, 94200 Kuching Sarawak

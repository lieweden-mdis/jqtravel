About Us

In 1987, Jia Quan's grandfather founded a Malaysian attractions company, capitalizing on the
country's diverse natural beauty. In 2020, Jia Quan inherited the business, but faced financial
losses due to travel restrictions. Seeking solutions, he partnered with Jacky, Eden, and Zhe Kai,
rebranded the company as J&Q Travel, and will launch a website to promote Malaysian tourism.

Vision

To be the most trusted and popular brand in the travel industry, promoting
cross-cultural understanding and friendships around the world by creating
memorable, surprising and inspiring journeys for every traveller, giving them a deep sense of the fascination of cultures around the world and inspiring
a passion for exploration and discovery.

Mission

Our mission is to connect people with people, people with nature,
and people with culture by providing exceptional travel experiences.
We are committed to providing our customers with safe, convenient and
enriching journeys that not only meet their expectations, but exceed
them. Our team will take care of every traveller with dedication and
passion, professionalism and innovative services to create a meaningful
trip with fond memories and lasting friendships.

Objectives

1. Provide a diverse and high-quality range of travel destinations and experiences to meet the needs and preferences of different travellers.
2. EnPromotesure safe and comfortable travel so that travellers can enjoy their journeys wholeheartedly.
3. sustainable tourism practices that respect and protect the natural environment and cultural heritage of the destination.
4. To develop a team of professional tour guides to provide travellers with a wealth of knowledge and enjoyable experiences.
5. To continuously innovate and improve to meet the changing needs and expectations of travellers.

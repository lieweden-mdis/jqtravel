Tour Detail

Le’ One – Kokol Elf, Kota Kinabalu, Sabah

09:00a.m – 07:30p.m

Description

Le' One Kokol Elf is a luxurious eco-resort located in Kokol Hill, Menggatal, Sabah, Malaysia. Perched atop a hill with breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes, including the city of Kota Kinabalu and the South China Sea, Le' One Kokol Elf offers guests a serene retreat amidst nature.

The resort is known for its unique and eco-friendly accommodations, which include traditional Malaysian huts, treehouses, and villas built with sustainable materials and designed to blend harmoniously with the natural surroundings. Each accommodation option provides modern amenities and comforts while allowing guests to immerse themselves in the tranquility of the environment.

One of the highlights of Le' One Kokol Elf is its infinity pool, which offers panoramic views of the lush hills and valleys below. Guests can relax and unwind while enjoying the stunning sunset or stargazing at night.

The resort also offers a range of outdoor activities and experiences for guests to enjoy, including guided nature walks, birdwatching, and cultural performances. Additionally, guests can indulge in authentic Malaysian cuisine at the on-site restaurant, which sources fresh ingredients from local farmers and fishermen.

Le' One Kokol Elf is a popular destination for couples seeking a romantic getaway, families looking for adventure and relaxation, and nature enthusiasts wanting to explore the beauty of Sabah's countryside. Whether you're seeking adventure or tranquility, Le' One Kokol Elf provides an unforgettable experience amidst the natural splendor of Kokol Hill.

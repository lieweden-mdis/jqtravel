5 Days 4 Nights Package

Overview

Sabah and Sarawak are two unique and fascinating states in the eastern region of Malaysia. Sabah is known for its magnificent natural landscapes, including the famous Mount Kinabalu and rich marine life resources that attract countless adventurers and nature lovers. Sarawak, on the other hand, is known for its diverse cultural heritage and intriguing traditions, where travellers can explore traditional villages, take part in cultural activities, or experience exciting adventure tours. Each of these two states has its own unique characteristics and offers visitors a rich and varied travel experience that will keep them coming back for more.



Day 1: Arrive in Sabah

Morning:

Upon arrival in Sabah, proceed to your accommodation location to check in.


Evening:

Dinner:Savor Sabah cuisine at a local restaurant and try local specialties.

Day 2: Explore Sabah

Morning:

Breakfast: Get ready for a day of adventure with a hearty breakfast.

Mount Kinabalu: Head to the famous Mount Kinabalu to enjoy its breathtaking scenery. If time permits, you can choose to hike or participate in other outdoor activities.


Afternoon:

Lunch: Have lunch in a surrounding restaurant or cafe.

Le’ One – Kokol Elf: Visit Le’ One – Kokol Elf and enjoy stunning mountain views and cozy surroundings.


Evening:

Dinner:Return to the city and have dinner at a local restaurant to end a pleasant day.

Day 3: Head to Sarawak

Morning:

Breakfast: After having breakfast, get ready to travel to Sarawak.

Transfer: Travel to Sarawak by plane or other means of transportation.


Afternoon:

Sarawak Cultural Village: Upon arrival, go to Sarawak Cultural Village to learn about Sarawak’s cultural heritage and traditional lifestyle.

Kuching Cat Museum: Visit the Kuching Cat Museum and explore various cat-related exhibits and stories.


Evening:

Dinner: Savor Sarawakian cuisine at a local restaurant and try local specialties.

Return trip: Return to your accommodation to rest and relax.

Day 4: Sarawak Adventure

Morning:

Breakfast: After breakfast, get ready for a new day of adventure.

Jong’s Crocodile Farm: Go to Jong’s Crocodile Farm to get up close and personal with crocodiles and learn about these mysterious creatures.


Afternoon:

Lunch: Have lunch in a surrounding restaurant or cafe.

After lunch: Continue to visit Jong’s Crocodile Farm, or choose other local attractions to explore based on personal interests.


At Night:

Dinner: Enjoy dinner at a restaurant in Kuching to reflect on the wonderful experience of the day.

Day Trip Package

Overview

Legoland focuses on creating parent-child experiences, with unique designs and activities that appeal to family members of all ages. Families can participate in a variety of interactive games and activities that promote cooperation and communication, and work together to solve problems, build LEGO models or participate in challenges. At the same time, Legoland provides a variety of rides for visitors of different ages. From play areas suitable for young children to exciting roller coasters, every family member can find something suitable for themselves. In addition, Legoland also has a family activity area, providing facilities such as children's playgrounds, parent-child workshops and family dining areas, providing families with a comfortable and safe environment so that they can enjoy the fun of the park. Through regularly organized themed performances and entertainment activities, such as LEGO role play and stage plays, LEGOLAND brings joy and entertainment to families while increasing parent-child interaction and participation.



Morning:

Breakfast: Refuel with a delicious breakfast at a nearby cafe or restaurant.

Arrive at Legoland: Arrive at Legoland and purchase your tickets. Make sure to check opening times and maps in advance to plan your trip.

Explore themed areas: Start your adventure! Explore the different themed areas, such as the Castle Area, Star Wars Area, and more, and look out for fun attractions and LEGO models.

Try rides: Try some rides, such as roller coasters, carousels, etc. Try to book your tickets in advance to avoid long queues.

Gallery




Afternoon：

Lunch: Have lunch at a restaurant or snack bar inside Legoland. Choose some fast food or light meals to fill your stomach quickly without delaying your play time.

Interactive Activities: Participate in interactive activities such as a LEGO engineering challenge or build your own LEGO model. These activities allow you to experience the fun of Lego more deeply.

Keep playing: Enjoy the remaining rides and attractions. Take photos and share your LEGO adventures with family and friends!

Gallery




Night ：

Dinner: Have dinner at Legoland or a nearby restaurant. Reflect on your day and talk about your favorite attractions and activities.

Departing Legoland: At the end of a fun day, take transportation back to your residence or hotel.

Pantai Cahaya Bulan




Tour Detail

Location: Pantai Cahaya Bulan, 15350, Kota Bharu, Kelantan
Operation Time: Open all day

Description

Pantai Cahaya Bulan, often referred to as PCB or Moonlight Beach, is one of the most popular beaches in Kelantan, Malaysia. Located just a short drive from Kota Bharu, the capital city, PCB stretches along the coast of the South China Sea, offering stunning views of the ocean and sandy shores.

The beach is renowned for its tranquil atmosphere and natural beauty, making it a favorite destination for both locals and tourists alike. Visitors can enjoy leisurely strolls along the shoreline, relax under the shade of coconut trees, or soak up the sun on the soft, golden sands. Pantai Cahaya Bulan is also a popular spot for picnics, beach games, and water sports such as swimming and jet-skiing.

One of the highlights of PCB is its spectacular sunsets, which paint the sky in vibrant hues of orange and pink, creating a magical ambiance. Additionally, the beach is lined with food stalls and small cafes offering delicious local snacks and refreshing beverages, allowing visitors to indulge in Malaysia's culinary delights while taking in the scenic views.

Overall, Pantai Cahaya Bulan is a picturesque and tranquil retreat where visitors can unwind, connect with nature, and enjoy the beauty of the Malaysian coastline.

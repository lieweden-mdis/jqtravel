Jonker Street




Tour Detail

Location:75200 Malacca
Operation Time: 09:00a.m – 12:00a.m

Description

Jonker Street is one of the most famous streets in Malacca, Malaysia. It is located in the center of Malacca and is one of the cultural centers and tourist attractions of this historical city. The street is famous for its unique architecture, traditional handicrafts, food and night markets, attracting tourists from all over the world.

The history of Jonker Street can be traced back to the colonial period of Malacca, when it was the commercial center of the Dutch and Portuguese colonists. Today, the street retains many historic buildings, including colorful shophouses, ancient temples and temples, showcasing Malacca's rich and diverse cultural heritage.

One of the main attractions of Jonker Street is its famous night market, where visitors can sample a variety of local delicacies and buy handicrafts, souvenirs and antiques. In addition, shops on both sides of the street also sell a variety of traditional medicines, tea, clothing and jewelry, providing visitors with a rich shopping experience.

In addition to shopping and food, Jonker Street also has many historical monuments and cultural attractions for tourists to explore, such as Cheng Hoon Teng Temple and Kampung Kling Mosque. This has made Jonker Street a vibrant and diverse tourist destination, attracting tourists from all over the world to experience Malacca’s unique cultural charm.

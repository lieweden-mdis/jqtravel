Perak
Perak, Malaysia's "Land of Grace," on the west coast, offers a rich blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. From historic towns like Kuala Kangsar to bustling Ipoh, known for its cuisine and colonial architecture, Perak showcases Malaysia's heritage. With limestone caves, lakes, and forests, it's a haven for nature lovers and adventurers

Melaka
Melaka, is a historic city located in Malaysia, renowned for its rich cultural heritage and strategic maritime position. It was once a vital trading port, attracting merchants from around the world, including China, India, and Europe. Today, Melaka is celebrated for its well-preserved colonial architecture, diverse cuisine, and vibrant blend of Malay, Chinese, Indian, and European influences.

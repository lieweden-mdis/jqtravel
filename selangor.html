Selangor
Selangor is one of the 13 states in Malaysia, located on the west coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Selangor is renowned for its vibrant mix of urban and rural areas, modern infrastructure, cultural diversity, and economic significance, being home to numerous industries and commercial centers. It also boasts beautiful natural attractions making it a popular destination for both tourists and locals alike.

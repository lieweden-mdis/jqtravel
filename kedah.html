Kedah
Kedah is a state situated in the northern region of Peninsular Malaysia, known as the "Rice Bowl of Malaysia" due to its extensive paddy fields. It's famous for its traditional Malay culture, handicrafts, and delicious local cuisine, making it a charming destination for travelers seeking both natural beauty and cultural experiences.

Eagle Square is one of the most famous landmarks and one of the main tourist attractions in Langkawi, Malaysia.

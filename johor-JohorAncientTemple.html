Description

Johor has many ancient temples, one of the well-known ones is the Johor Bahru Old Chinese Temple, also known as The Temple of Five Deities. Built in the 19th century, this temple is one of the oldest religious buildings in Johor and has a long history and rich cultural heritage.

The architectural style of the ancient temple in Johor Bahru showcases traditional Chinese architectural art, including ornate roofs, colorful murals and exquisite wood carvings. There are five different gods enshrined in the temple, representing the patron saints of the five directions respectively. People come to pray for health, happiness and good luck.

In addition to religious activities, the ancient temple in Johor Bahru is also a venue for cultural activities. It regularly holds various traditional festival celebrations, attracting many believers and tourists. This ancient temple is an important cultural heritage of Johor and an attraction that attracts tourists to learn about local culture and history.

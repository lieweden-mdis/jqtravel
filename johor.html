Johor
Johor is a state located in the southern part of Peninsular Malaysia, bordering Singapore to the south. Johor Bahru, the capital city, is a bustling metropolis with modern amenities and historical landmarks. The state is also famous for its theme parks, nature reserves, and traditional Malay villages, making it a popular destination for both local and international tourists.

Attractions

Johor Ancient Temple
The state of Johor is home to many ancient temples, one of the well-known ones is the Johor Ancient Temple, also known as The Temple of Five Deities. Built in the 19th century, this temple is one of the oldest religious buildings in Johor Bahru and has a long history and rich cultural heritage.....

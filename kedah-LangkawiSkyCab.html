Langkawi SkyCab




Tour Detail

Location:Jalan Telaga Tujuh, 07000 Langkawi, Kedah
Operation Time: 09:30a.m – 06:00p.m

Description

Langkawi SkyCab is one of the main tourist attractions in Langkawi Island, Kedah, Malaysia. Considered the longest single-cable aerial tramway in the world, this cable car system offers a breathtaking panoramic scenic experience.

This SkyCab system starts from the city of Uluru (Ulu Melaka) near Limbang town (Kuah) and ends at the top of the Mat Cincang mountain range on Langkawi island. The cable car cabins along the way provide visitors with spectacular 360-degree panoramic views of Langkawi’s beautiful coastline, lush tropical rainforest and surrounding archipelago landscapes.

After arriving at the top of Mat Cincang, visitors can enjoy even more spectacular views from the viewing platform and have the opportunity to explore the surrounding natural landscape and walking trails. The Langkawi SkyCab is a great way for visitors to explore this beautiful Malaysian holiday destination, offering unforgettable experiences and unforgettable scenery.

Description

Putuo Village is the only tourist attraction in Southern Malaysia that combines Buddhist culture and leisurely atmosphere. In addition to making a pilgrimage to Buddhist culture, guests can also experience the natural beauty away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Located in a peaceful bamboo forest on the outskirts of Kulai, Johor, Malaysia, Putuo Village covers an area of 43 acres and is a haven away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

This village is famous for its tranquil Buddhist cultural scenic spot - Bodhi Harbor, and the dense bamboo forest innovative leisure area - Zizhu Valley. Putuo Village houses the largest indoor statue of Zhunti Guanyin in Southeast Asia, proving its deep connection with the Buddhist faith. For nearly two decades, the village has been a spiritual retreat, attracting visitors seeking peace and tranquility.

isitors can stroll through picturesque bamboo forests, admire exquisite Buddhist architecture, and taste delicious vegetarian food. Putuo Village blends cultural exploration with nature immersion, allowing visitors to gain a deeper understanding of Buddhist traditions. In addition, cultural performances and workshops are held in the village to add joy and color to visitors.
